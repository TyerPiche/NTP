Classic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce and serve.
