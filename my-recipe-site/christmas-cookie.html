Christmas Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3.75 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup of butter softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together in a medium bowl; set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth.
  3. Beat first egg into butter mixture. Beat second egg into mixture along with vanilla extract; add flour mixture and stir until dough is just combined.
  4. Wrap dough and chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease two cookie sheets.
  6. Roll out dough on a clean floured surface to 1/4-inch thickness. Cut out shapes using Christmas cookie cutters; arrange them on the prepared cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes, until set and golden.
  8. Use your fingers or a fork to crimp. Loosely tent with plastic wrap and refrigerate while you preheat the oven to 425°F.
  9. Roll out dough on a clean floured surface to 1/4-inch thickness. Cut out shapes using Christmas cookie cutters; arrange them on the prepared cookie sheets.
  10. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. Allow to cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
