Ingredients

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup white rum
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup soda water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the mint leaves, white rum, lime juice, and sugar. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the soda water to the pitcher and stir gently.
  3. Serve over ice cubes, garnished with additional mint leaves if desired.
  4. Serve over ice, garnished with additional fruit slices if desired.
