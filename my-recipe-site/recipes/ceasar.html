Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, Parmesan cheese, croutons, and Caesar dressing.
  2. Toss until the salad is well coated with the dressing.
  3. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
