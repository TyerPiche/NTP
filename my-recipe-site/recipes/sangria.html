Ingredients

  • 1 bottle red wine
  • 1/4 cup brandy
  • 1/4 cup orange liqueur
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 lime, sliced
  • 1 cup soda water

Instructions

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the red wine, brandy, orange liqueur, and sugar. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add the sliced orange, lemon, and lime to the pitcher.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Just before serving, add the soda water to the pitcher and stir gently.
  5. Serve over ice, garnished with additional fruit slices if desired.
