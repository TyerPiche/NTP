Berry overnight Oats

  • Breakfast
  • Dinner
  • Desserts

Ingredients

  • 16oz frozen mixed berries, thawed
  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup plant-based milk or water
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Combine thawed berries,oats, milk and salt in large bowl. Mix well
  2. Cover and refrigerate overnight or 8hrs
