Ingredients

  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup fram flour/chickpea flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground tumeric
  • 1-2 green chillies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons freshly chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 5-6 tablespoons of water
  • oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl shick together the flour, baking powder, salt, cumin and turmeric until combined.
  2. Add the chilli, cilantro, lemon juice and water. Mix into thick batter.
  3. Add the sliced onions to the bowl and mix until well coated.
  4. Heat oil for frying in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Once the oil is hot, drop spoonfuls of the onion mixture into the oil.
  6. Fry until golden brown and crispy, about 4-5 minutes.
  7. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  8. Serve hot with chutney or yogurt dip.
  9. Enjoy your homemade onion bhajis!
