Brownie Cookies

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp of butter, melted and cooled slightly
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 3/4 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp. of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 box of brownie mix
  • 3 tbsp. water
  • 1.3 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1.4 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. In a large bowl, mix together melted butter, flour, salt, vanilla extract, cocoa powder, and eggs until well combined.Line 2 medium sheet trays with parchment paper and grease with cooking spray.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together butter, flour, salt, vanilla, and cocoa powder. Add eggs one at a time, whisking for 1 minute after each egg.
  4. Mix in brownie mix and water, then fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop cookies onto sheet trays, using about 2 tablespoons batter for each and separating them two inches on the tray. Refrigerate for 20 minutes.
  6. Bake cookies for about 12 minutes, just until they begin forming a crust on top. They will continue to cook slightly as they cool. If you like crispier cookies, bake them for an extra minute. Let cool 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
