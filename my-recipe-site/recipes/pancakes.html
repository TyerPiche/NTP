Classic Pancakes

  • Breakfast
  • Dinner
  • Desserts

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook another 2 min.
